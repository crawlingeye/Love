Rosalia Scalera

Love

Wandering entity that inhabits this earth

sighing on the lips of distant lovers.

Dressed in roses and sapphires,

you beg in deserted streets,

hidden in the confined spaces

of a ghost town.

With your fatal grip

You welcome the cry of a farewell

and let the weary limbs

Rest on your breasts
